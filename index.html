Looking for the perfect vacation getaway? Discover luxury living at its finest in this stunning 3-bedroom, 2.5-bath townhome located in the exclusive Lost Key Golf & Beach Club Villas in Perdido Key, Florida. This three-story home offers a spacious open-concept design on the second level, seamlessly connecting the gathering room, dining area, and modern kitchen, with easy access to a covered lanai for indoor-outdoor living.

Whether you're a golf enthusiast or a beach lover, Lost Key has it all. Enjoy access to the championship Lost Key Golf Course, complete with a pro shop, or relax at the exclusive Gulf-front Beach Club, featuring a beachfront pool and restaurant with breathtaking views. Getting to the beach is a breeze with the seasonal shuttle, allowing you to soak up the sun on the white sand shores of Perdido Key.
